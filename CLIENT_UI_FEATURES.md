# SkyIQ - Client UI Features Guide

## What You Can Do in the SkyIQ Platform

This guide shows all the features you can control directly from the user interface.

---

## 📞 Call Dashboard

### View Your Call History
- **See All Calls**: View a complete list of all your incoming and outgoing calls
- **Call Information Displayed**:
  - Date and time of the call
  - Phone number of caller/recipient
  - How long the call lasted
  - Call status (Completed, Missed, or Failed)
  - AI-generated summary of the conversation
  
### Search & Filter Calls
- **Search**: Find specific calls by typing:
  - Phone number
  - Contact name
  - Keywords from the summary
- **Filter by Status**: Show only:
  - Completed calls
  - Missed calls
  - Failed calls
- **Sort**: Organize your calls by:
  - Date & Time (newest first)
  - Call duration
  - Status

### View Call Details
- **Click Any Call** to see:
  - Full conversation transcript
  - Complete AI summary
  - Call recording (if available)
  - All call metadata
  
### Manage Individual Calls
- **Add Notes**: Write custom notes about each call
- **Edit Notes**: Update notes anytime
- **Save Changes**: Keep your notes for future reference
- **Delete Calls**: Remove call records you don't need

### Real-Time Updates
- **Live Notifications**: See new calls appear instantly
- **Automatic Transcripts**: Transcripts appear in real-time as calls complete
- **Connection Status**: Know when you're connected or disconnected

## 🤖 AI Voice Agent Configuration

### Make Outbound Calls

#### Single Call
- **Enter Phone Number**: Type in the number you want to call
- **Initiate Call**: Click to start the call immediately
- **Track Status**: Watch the call progress in real-time

#### Batch Calls
- **Upload CSV File**: Upload a file with multiple phone numbers
- **Review Numbers**: See all numbers that will be called
- **Start Batch**: Initiate calls to everyone on your list
- **Monitor Progress**: Track which calls are complete

### Customize Your AI Agent

#### Set Initial Greeting
- **First Message**: Write what your AI says when the call connects
- **Preview**: See exactly what callers will hear
- **Update Anytime**: Change the greeting whenever you want

#### Configure AI Behavior
- **System Prompt**: Define how your AI agent should behave:
  - Personality and tone
  - Knowledge and expertise
  - How to handle different situations
  - What information to provide
- **Character Limit**: Write detailed instructions (up to 2000+ characters)
- **Save & Apply**: Update your agent configuration instantly

### Manage Saved Prompts
- **Save Configurations**: Store up to 3 different prompt setups
- **Name Your Prompts**: Give each configuration a descriptive name
- **Quick Load**: Switch between configurations with one click
- **Delete Prompts**: Remove configurations you no longer need

## 🏢 Business Profile

### Manage Business Information

#### Basic Details
- **Business Name**: Set your company name
- **Email Address**: Add your business email
- **Phone Number**: List your business phone
- **Address**: Add your business location
- **Save Changes**: Update all information at once

#### Business Description
- **Write Description**: Explain what your business does
- **AI Integration**: Your AI agent uses this to represent you accurately
- **Edit Anytime**: Update as your business evolves

### Brand Your Profile

#### Upload Logo
- **Click to Upload**: Select your business logo
- **Supported Formats**: PNG, JPG, SVG
- **Size Limit**: Up to 2MB
- **Preview**: See how it looks before saving
- **Update Logo**: Change your logo anytime
- **Where It Appears**:
  - Your profile page
  - Application header/navigation
  - Call dashboard

### Business Context Management

#### Upload Business Files
- **Add Documents**: Upload files about your business
- **Supported Types**:
  - PDF documents
  - Word files (DOCX)
  - Images (JPG, PNG)
  - Text files (TXT)
- **Size Limit**: Up to 5MB per file
- **What Happens**: Content is automatically extracted and given to your AI agent
- **View Files**: See all uploaded documents
- **Delete Files**: Remove files you don't need anymore

#### Add Website Links
- **Add URLs**: Enter your website or social media links
- **Automatic Scraping**: We automatically extract content from your website
- **AI Knowledge**: Your AI agent learns about your business from these links
- **Manage Links**: View all added links
- **Remove Links**: Delete links you no longer want included

#### Lead Files
- **Upload CSV**: Add lead lists for follow-up
- **Manage Leads**: View and organize your lead files
- **Delete Leads**: Remove lead files when done

## 🔗 Integration Settings

### Twilio Configuration
- **Account SID**: Enter your Twilio account identifier
- **Auth Token**: Add your Twilio authentication token
- **Phone Number**: Set your Twilio phone number
- **Test Connection**: Verify your credentials work
- **Save Settings**: Store your Twilio configuration

### ElevenLabs Configuration
- **API Key**: Enter your ElevenLabs API key
- **Agent ID**: Set your conversational AI agent ID
- **Phone Number ID**: Configure your ElevenLabs phone number
- **Update Agent**: Save your ElevenLabs settings
- **Test Integration**: Verify the connection works

### Cal.com Configuration (Optional)
- **API Key**: Add your Cal.com API key
- **Event Type**: Select which calendar events to use
- **Timezone**: Set your preferred timezone
- **Enable Booking**: Allow AI to schedule meetings during calls
